Purpouse

The purpose will be increase the visibility of my business, to be a showcase, be a communicative way with the customer and got a easiest way to booking and reservation.

Audience

Who are the target customers?

People that would like to start rafting or already do rafting, that have minimum of physical conditions, like water adventure and are at least lower middle class.

What do they want?

Learn or practice rafting with friends or a personal instructure.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

Where rafting occurs, how we will provide their security, the protocols to do rafting and the information about our company. In addition, they can do booking and reservation.
